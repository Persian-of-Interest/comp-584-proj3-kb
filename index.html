Chelo Kebab (چلوکباب)

Chelo Kebab is an Iranian dish consisting of steamed rice (chelow) and one of the many varieties of Iranian kebab. It is considered the national dish of Iran.

Fesenjan (فسنجان)

Fesenjān is a sweet and sour Iranian stew. The roots of this Persian delicacy trace back to Sassanid Persia's golden age. It is typically served over rice in the Iranian manner.

Ghormeh Sabzi (قورمه‌ سبزی)

Ghormeh Sabzi is an Iranian herb stew. It is a very commonly served dish in Iran. Ghormeh sabzi has different variants, which are based on the different types of beans and meat.

Zereshk Polo (زرشک پلو)

Zereshk Polo is a classic Iranian dish of saffron rice mixed with tart barberries (zereshk), commonly served with a side of saffron-infused chicken, known as zereshk polo ba morgh. It is a popular and elegant meal, often prepared for festive occasions and family gatherings.

Tahchin (ته چین)

Tahchin is an Iranian rice dish primarily consisting of rice, yogurt, eggs, saffron, and various types of meat.

Gheymeh (قیمه)

Gheymeh is an Iranian stew (khoresh) consisting of diced mutton, tomatoes, split peas, onion, and dried lime, garnished with golden, thinly sliced crispy potatoes. The stew is sometimes garnished with fried eggplant and is usually served with white rice (polow).

Beets (لبو)

It is a very popular Iranian street food snack, especially during the fall and winter months. Vendors in cities across Iran sell hot, steamed, sweet beets from wheeled carts, often served simply with a dash of sugar or syrup

Bandari Sausage (سوسیس بندری)

Sosis Bandari (translated as Port sausage) is an Iranian fast-food or street food, usually served in sandwich shops in Iran in the form of a sub or meal. It contains sausage, onion, tomato paste, ground chilli pepper and other spices.

Sabzi Polo with Fish (سبزی پلو با ماهی)

Sabzi polo is a Persian dish of rice and chopped herbs, usually served with fish. Iranians traditionally eat sabzi polo with fish for lunch on Nowruz, the Persian New Year.

Sholeh Zard (شله‌زرد)

Sholezard is a rice pudding composed of saffron, sugar, rose water, butter, cinnamon and cardamom.

Faloodeh Shirazi (فالوده شیرازی)

Faloodeh, is a traditional Iranian cold dessert similar to a sorbet. It consists of thin vermicelli-sized noodles made from starch in a semi-frozen syrup containing sugar and rose water. Faloodeh is often served with lime juice and sometimes ground pistachios.

Persian Style Baklava (باقلوا)

Persian baghlava is neither chunky or too gooey and is usually made with pistachios and skinless almonds along with ground cardamom and the sugar rose water, saffron syrup. The best baghlava in Iran is from the city of Yazd.
